📋 STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS

🧭 Priority Action Plan for 2025-26
Target: PRACHESTA-1 grade and top-3 nationally
  • 🎯 Priority #1 — Learning Outcomes (LO): Set target LO 130+ in 2025-26 (current 100). Focus on FLN saturation (G1-3), math/science teacher upskilling (G6-9), rigorous PARAKH sample prep. Even +30 LO points = grade upgrade.
  • 🏛️ Priority #2 — Governance Processes (GP): Digital attendance to 100%, on-time PRABANDH fund releases (<30 days), triple Vidyanjali registrations. Target GP 90+.
  • 👩‍🏫 Priority #3 — Teacher Education (TE&T): Arrest the decline. Audit minimum qualifications, accelerate Nishtha 4.0/5.0, ensure DIETs fully staffed. Restore UTKARSH/UTTAM-1.
  • 🏫 Maintain Equity Leadership: Track gender parity, CWSN integration, SC/ST/Minority outcomes monthly.
  • 📊 Establish PGI War-Room: Monthly review of all 70 indicators with district dashboards. Assign owners to 15-20 indicators with largest gaps.
  • 🔗 Benchmarking Visits: Send delegations to Chandigarh, Punjab, Kerala for cross-learning.
  • 📈 Stretch Target: PGI 720+ in 2025-26 (PRACHESTA-1, top-3). Needs +37 pts — well within reach given +91 this year.
